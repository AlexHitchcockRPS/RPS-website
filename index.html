CUE 3.0GO

Choose your level of assurance

OSM is a tiered assurance programme for live performance automation systems. Each tier blends intelligent triage, specialist engineering access and lifecycle planning — scaling from small venues to mission-critical cruise fleets. Not sure which fits? Talk to us.

OSM Ready

Performance Ready

The foundation package — access to specialist automation expertise, annual system health reviews and structured triage support.

Typical applications: small theatres, museums, visitor attractions, educational venues, small automation systems.

OSM Managed

Performance Managed

A proactive service providing operational oversight, maintenance planning and priority access to engineering expertise.

Typical applications: large theatres, cruise ships, arenas, corporate venues, regional attractions.

OSM Critical

Performance Assured

A comprehensive operational assurance programme for systems where downtime has significant operational impact.

Typical applications: cruise fleets, major attractions, broadcast facilities, high-profile theatres, mission-critical automation systems.
